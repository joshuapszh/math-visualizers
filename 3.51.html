Algorithm: The AC Method

Step 1: Check for Common Factors

Extract any common numbers or variables shared by ALL terms first. Set them outside a bracket.

Step 2: The Magic Number Puzzle

Look at the inner quadratic ax² + bx + c. You must find two numbers that:
• Multiply to equal the Target Product (a × c)
• Add to equal the Target Sum (b)

Step 3: Split the Middle Term

Rewrite the expression, replacing the middle 'bx' term with your two new magic numbers attached to the variable.

Step 4: Factor by Grouping

Group the four terms into two pairs (the first two, and the last two). Extract the common factor from each pair.

Step 5: Final Extraction

You will be left with identical brackets. Extract this matching bracket to get your final factorised answer!
