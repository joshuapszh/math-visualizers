Real-World Application Steps:
1. Read carefully & draw diagram.
2. Define your variable (e.g., let x = ...).
3. Form a quadratic equation.
4. Solve and reject impossible answers.
The Substitution Trick:
If you solved 2x² - 11x + 12 = 0,
you can instantly solve 2(n-1)² - 11(n-1) + 12 = 0
by substituting (n-1) in place of x.
