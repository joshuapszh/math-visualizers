Steps to draw the graph of a quadratic equation:

  1. Construct a table of values of x and y for the quadratic equation.
  2. Choose a suitable scale. The scale used on the x-axis does not have to be the same as the y-axis. The scale chosen should allow for the largest possible graph to be drawn for maximum accuracy.
  3. Plot the points on a graph paper and join them up to form a smooth curve.
  4. Label your graph with the equation of the curve.
NOTE! When drawing quadratic graphs, it is important that you join the points up with a smooth curve instead of a series of straight lines.
