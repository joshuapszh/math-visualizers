📐 Special Algebraic Identities — Quick Reference

Perfect Square (Sum)
(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
Perfect Square (Difference)
(a − b)² = a² − 2ab + b²
Difference of Two Squares
(a + b)(a − b) = a² − b²
