Reference: The Zero Product Principle

The Principle:
If P and Q are factors of an algebraic expression such that PQ = 0, then P = 0 or Q = 0.
Steps to Solve:
1. Write in general form (LHS = 0).
2. Factorise the LHS.
3. Set each factor equal to 0.
4. Solve the new equations.
