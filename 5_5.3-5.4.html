Chapter 5 Targets

  • Use quadratic equations and graphs to solve maths problems in real-world contexts.
  • Solve complex equations graphically by finding points of intersection.

Reference: Solving Equations Graphically

Finding Roots (y = 0):
To solve ax² + bx + c = 0, find where the curve cuts the x-axis.
Intersection Method:
To solve ax² + bx + c = k, draw the horizontal line y = k.
The x-coordinates of the intersections are your solutions!
